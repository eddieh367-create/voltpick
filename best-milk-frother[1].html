What to Look For When Buying a Milk Frother

Motor power — more powerful motors froth faster and create denser foam. Look for at least 15,000 RPM. The Zulay is among the most powerful handheld frothers available.

Milk compatibility — make sure your frother works with plant-based milks if you use them. Most good frothers handle oat, almond and soy milk well.

Ease of cleaning — handheld wand frothers are the easiest to clean. Automatic jug frothers require washing the jug which takes slightly longer.
