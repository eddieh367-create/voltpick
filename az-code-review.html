What Exactly is The AZ Code?

The AZ Code is a digital training program that teaches you how to make money through Amazon — specifically through their affiliate program, Amazon Associates. It was created to give complete beginners a step-by-step system for earning commissions by recommending Amazon products online. No prior experience required.

I went through the entire program to give you an honest assessment. The short version — it delivers what it promises for beginners. It is not a get-rich-quick scheme, it does not promise overnight riches, and it is surprisingly practical. If you are willing to put in the work the system is solid.

What is Inside The AZ Code?

The program is structured as a series of video modules that walk you through the entire Amazon affiliate process from scratch. The content quality is genuinely good. The explanations are clear, the steps are logical, and there are no confusing technical leaps. Does The AZ Code Actually Work?

This is the question everyone wants answered honestly. The system works — but only if you follow it consistently. The people who buy The AZ Code and do nothing with it will earn nothing. What The AZ Code does well is remove the guesswork. Instead of spending months figuring out what to do you have a clear path from day one. That alone is worth the price for most beginners. At $37 it is also one of the most affordable legitimate affiliate training programs available.
