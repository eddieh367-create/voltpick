Why You Need a Privacy Screen in 2026

If you work in cafes, airports, trains, co-working spaces or open plan offices, other people can see your screen. This is not just uncomfortable — it is a genuine security and privacy risk. Financial data, client information, confidential documents, personal emails and passwords are all visible to anyone sitting at an angle to your screen.

A laptop privacy screen filter uses micro-louver technology to block side-angle views — anyone not sitting directly in front of your screen sees only a dark or black screen. From your position directly in front the screen appears normal with only a slight reduction in brightness.
