Drilling into bathroom tiles is a commitment — and a risk. Tiles crack, grout gets damaged, and landlords charge for holes at the end of a tenancy. No drill adhesive shower caddies solve all of these problems while still providing secure, permanent-feeling storage.

How to Install a No Drill Shower Caddy Correctly

The most common reason adhesive caddies fail is incorrect installation. Follow these steps for a secure hold that lasts:

  • Clean the surface thoroughly with rubbing alcohol and let it dry completely
  • Make sure the surface temperature is above 50°F — cold surfaces reduce adhesion
  • Press the caddy firmly against the surface for 30 to 60 seconds
  • Wait a full 24 hours before placing any items on the caddy
  • Do not overload — stay within the weight limit specified on the packaging
